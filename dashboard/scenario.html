These are stress tests, not forecasts. GFC and COVID scenarios show the exact indicator z-scores recorded during those episodes — applied to the current framework to answer: "what would the MRS read if conditions matched that period today?" The Custom scenario and Playground let you define your own thesis. All computation is live in your browser using the same weights as the production model.
GFC 2008 December 2008 · trough

Credit markets froze, equity markets fell −57%, Fed cut to near-zero. The worst US financial crisis since the Depression — and the only episode where the MRS reached Contraction without ambiguity.

COVID 2020 March 2020 · trough

Pandemic lockdowns triggered the sharpest output collapse in history. SPY fell −34% in 23 days. A brief but severe Contraction — primarily a Growth and Stress shock rather than a credit crisis.
