    What is the MRS?

    A single monthly read on the US macro-financial environment from 13 indicators across five pillars — Growth (30%), Credit (20%), Market Stress (20%), Inflation (15%), Liquidity/Rates (15%). Each is scored in standard-deviation (z) units vs. its own history; the weighted average is the composite. 0 = neutral, higher = better.

    Regimes change only after a reading persists two consecutive months, so the label is deliberately slow to flip. See About for the full method.
